Kale Soup

Description

A simple soup made from kale, italian sausage, chicken broth, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Kale, 2 pounds
  • 2 pounds mild italian sausage
  • 2 quarts chicken broth
  • 3 pounds potatoes
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Cook italian sausage and onion until done
  2. Add chicken broth and bring to a simmer
  3. Add potatoes and cook until soup thickens, 30 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and add kale, stir to combine for 5 minutes
