Bruce Roast

Description

A simple beef roast named after the creator, Bruce Keller.

Ingredients

  • Sliced roast beef
  • 1 can mushroom gravy
  • 1 can brown gravy
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions

Preparation

  1. Cook mushrooms and onions to desired doneness
  2. Add all ingredients to a slow cooker
  3. Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 6 hours
  4. Serve as open-faced sandwiches!
